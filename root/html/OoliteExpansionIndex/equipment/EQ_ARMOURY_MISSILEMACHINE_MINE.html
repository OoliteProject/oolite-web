Equipment Missile Machine

The missile machine is fitted in the ships hold along with a pylon-mounted auto-loader. When triggered it manufactures standard or ECM hardened missiles from materials stored in the cargo hold. For each ton of alloys and radioactives plus a little fuel it can produce up to three missiles, and with the addition of a ton of computers these are upgraded to ECM-hardened weapons.
