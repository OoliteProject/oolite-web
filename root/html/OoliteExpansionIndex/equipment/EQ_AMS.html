Equipment Anti-Missile System

A hull-mounted defensive system that augments the missile analyser by launching a ballistic dart to intercept and destroy any detected ship-targeted non-standard missile. The launcher can be loaded with up to 6 darts, which can be restocked individually or to full capacity.
