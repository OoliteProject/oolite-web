Equipment Anti-Sapper Equipment

Limit the point-blank damage caused by Sapper weapons to 2x instead of 5x but get damaged 10 times more than other equipments. SG ship variants (like Asp SG) usually hold one by default so you should not use your Sapper against these at all, Sniper Gun is equally effective while use less energy and make much less heat.
