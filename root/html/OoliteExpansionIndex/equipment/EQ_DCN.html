Equipment Damage Control Node

Exclusive to the Caduceus class ships, this cybernetic, self-regenerating nano-forge module interfaces with the onboard AI and cybersystems, and aids the crew in the in-flight repairs of most installed equipment, manufacturing replacement parts and components on-the-fly. This is not a fail-proof tool; Repairs may take some time to complete, not always on the first try, and not all equipment is repairable by use of this unit.
