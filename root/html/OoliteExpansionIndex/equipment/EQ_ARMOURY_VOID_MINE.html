Equipment Void Bomb

A very dangerous bomb containing a suspended micro-singularity. When detonated the suspension is lifted and all entities within range are drawn toward the singularity. The singularity itself tends to be unstable, and explosively self-destructs after a short time, dealing damage to all things trapped close to it.
