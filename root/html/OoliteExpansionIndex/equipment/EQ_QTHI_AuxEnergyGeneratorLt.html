Equipment Light Auxiliary Energy Generator

Quirium Tech Heavy Industries High-Tech Light Auxiliary Energy Generator to use Witchspace fuel for generating energy. Automatic kickoff at critical energy level. Installed directly into the Witchspace engines, making it necessary to alternate. Needs a little energy for the barrier to keep the witchspace fuel from breaking out into engines. Generates no heat though.
