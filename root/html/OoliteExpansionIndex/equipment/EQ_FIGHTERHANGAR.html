Equipment Fighter Hangar

Convert your ship to a well equipped carrier by allocating space for large supply rooms, which can handle 80 fighters at once. Need a very big ship over 8000t mass which can not fit into regular docks, like Andromeda or Condor. Contain self-repair systems which make the hangar undamageable but not the stored fighters.
