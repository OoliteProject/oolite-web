Equipment Emergency Charge Device

From the Aquarian Shipbuilding Corp's Ships Systems Dept, a pylon-mounted device to give an emergency boost to the ships energy reserves when triggered. NOTE: this does not enhance the maximum available energy of the ship, any excess energy above normal capacity is dissipated.
