Equipment Escape Pod Plus

An upgrade to a normal escape pod, and a huge improvement over the standard escape vehicle fitted into all ships. This pod provides a manual launch facility, and further reduces the amount of any repurchase required in the event of ship destruction, over that provided by a normal escape pod. Also includes an upgraded emergency beacon which reduces the amount of time for a rescue to less than 12 hours, and allows for the recovery of around 50% of any cargo.
