Equipment Multipass

If plugged into your ship's transponder by the hackers on this asteroid the software on this device browses through GalCop's central database, clearing your legal record. You also have to pay for the sub-ether broadband connection; the longer your legal record, the more money it'll cost (1000-7000 credits). You will be notified when the process is finished.
