Equipment Sniper Gun

The strong and long range (20km) Sniper Gun fit into ships only with a special big gun mount on the hull. Deliver almost as much damage as a Military Laser, use less energy thanks to the costly high-tech components, moreover heat up 2 times slower due to placed outside of the hull. Can mine asteroids.
