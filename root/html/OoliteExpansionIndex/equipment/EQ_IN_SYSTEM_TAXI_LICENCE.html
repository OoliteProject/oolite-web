Equipment In-System Taxi Comms

Join Taxi Galactica's in-system taxi drivers division. Deliver customers from one station to another in-system. Buy this device and you'll receive local delivery offers right to you Comms while cruising. When you get an offer, you have one minute to decide to take it or not. If you want to take it, just prime this small gadget and activate it.
