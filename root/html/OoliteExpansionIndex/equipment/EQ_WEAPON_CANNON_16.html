Equipment ColtMaster Behemoth Plasma Web

This cannon load hot plasma in web form into twin artillery barrels to get longer range (4 km), lower heat and double fire rate than a Plasma Web, which mean double energy regeneration in each seconds also. The best weapon to kick out pilots from any ships in Escape Pods and also better in caused damage/seconds than any other cannons below behemoths. Need a ship over 8.000t (Behemoth or BigShips).
