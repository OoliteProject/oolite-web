Equipment Singel-Use Witchdrive

The Single-Use Witchdrive is a smaller variation of the standard Witchdrive, which is build into many types of ships. It still manages to create a Wormhole by overcharging its much smaller than standard Quirium Coils. Sadly they are burned out after one use. Replacement coils are fairly available in nearly all but the most lowtech systems.
