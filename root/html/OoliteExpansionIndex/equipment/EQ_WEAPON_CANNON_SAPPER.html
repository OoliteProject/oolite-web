Equipment Sapper

Sapper is a Sniper Gun in overdrive mode: deliver 5x more damage at point blank range than at max. range but use 5x energy and make double heat. The 5x hit is within 500m, 2x at 2km, 1x at 20km. Cause some damage when the shot narrowly misses the target. Can mine asteroids but unmask your cloak when fire and can not be separated.
