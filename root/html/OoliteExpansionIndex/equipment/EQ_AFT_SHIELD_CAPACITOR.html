Equipment Aft Shield Capacitor

SERS(Shield Energy Reinforcement Systems) Corp. provides an aft shield capacitor for your ship. Stores unused shield energy and discharges it into the aft shield when it drops below the amount stored. Capacity rating is 1/2 standard shield maximum. Operation is fully automatic for ease of use in combat.
