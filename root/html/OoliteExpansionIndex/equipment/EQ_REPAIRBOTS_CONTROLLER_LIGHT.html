Equipment Self-Repair System Light

An enhancement on the Aquarian Repair Bots technology, this is an integrated ship system for damage control and repair whilst in-flight. It can systematically detect and resolve defects and damage to most ships systems over time without crew intervention. Light version, takes half the space but stores half the charges
