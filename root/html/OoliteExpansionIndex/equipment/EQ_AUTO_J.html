Equipment Overdrive

We will re-route some Jump circuitry, linking it to your vehicle's velocity-controller. When full speed is set, J-drive will engage in a limited mode, similar to an injector, but silent, fluid, faster, and without the attendant fuel-waste. We call it the O-drive. To disengage it, decelerate to below your normal top speed.
