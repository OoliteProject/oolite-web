Equipment Shield Equalizer

SERS(Shield Energy Reinforcement Systems) Corp. provides a shield equalizer for your ship with no efficiency losses. Transfers shield energy automatically from high to low shield to prevent shield systems/equipment damage. Fully automatic for ease of use in combat. Due to high quality cross-connected energy transfer power couplings and being over-engineered there is more resistance to SHIELD system damage generally. A real plus that you won't get with the cheaper clones.
