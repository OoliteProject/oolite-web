Equipment JK Plasma Artillery Cannon

Thanks to the long artillery barrels the range of this plasma cannon is extended to 8 km. Does not need energy and cause critical damages as often as a Plasma Ray but produce much less heat so can fire continually much longer. Need a ship with at least 800t mass like Carriers, Kirin, Maelstrom or Serpent Class Cruiser.
