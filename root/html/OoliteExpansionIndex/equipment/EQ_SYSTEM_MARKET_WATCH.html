Equipment AMW_Unit

Tripsa Tel-com 'Autonomous Market Watch Unit'. While GalCop heavily encrypts Market Data to prevent price manuipulation, the AMW carefully applies bitshifts and fourier transforms to inform its user of changes in the local system main market. It can identify the commodity which has had a change in price and or availability and prompts the user to view their Market Datascreen.
