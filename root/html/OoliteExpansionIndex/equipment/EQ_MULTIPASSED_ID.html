Equipment Multi-Passed ID

The gang on this asteroid has salvaged a couple of transponders from wrecks of ships that vanished in this quite ... 'busy' system. For a very reasonable price they fix one to your ship, giving you a new legal identity. No questions asked. However some of these devices have been salvaged and passed between ships multiple times, so you never know to which legal records in GalCop's central database they might point...
