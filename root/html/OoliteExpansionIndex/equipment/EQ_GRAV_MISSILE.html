Equipment Graviton Missile

Releases a massive burst of graviton radiation, overloading shield generators with catastrophic results for the target. Guidance is provided by a trained trumble. Immune to any known ECM or EMP defence, the range of the weapon is limited to 5km by the visual acuity of the average trumble. When fired the trumble pilot will guide the missile towards the nearest Balrog Class Dreadnaught at 0.2 LM.
