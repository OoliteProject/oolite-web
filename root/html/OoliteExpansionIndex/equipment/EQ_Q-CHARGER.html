Equipment Q-Charger

Q-RaceTech has available a 'Q-Charger' Quirium Supercharging System for your ship. When throttle is over 95% the Q-Charger comes to life. Top speed is 2x max ship speed and fuel use per km is half that of a fuel injection system. Requires 0.1 ly of fuel every 30 seconds and 4 energy per second. A good bargain and great for dogfighting. Must have injectors installed before purchase and an energy unit installed to operate.
