Equipment Single-Use Witchdrive Spare Coils

A second set of coils for your Single-Use Witchdrive. This set enables your ship to create a second wormhole without maintenance in between. This is useful to leave systems without the means to repair your drive or to escape a Thargoid attack. This coils are ejected after the are burned out.
