Equipment Battle Gatling Laser

Three times stronger than a Gatling Laser but must cool down to zero to be usable again after overheat. Hold down the fire button when overheated to force the built-in cooler to dissipate the heat as fast as possible. Range is 10km only and need a ship over 800t like Carriers, Kirin, Maelstrom or Serpent Class Cruiser.
