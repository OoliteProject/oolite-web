Equipment Base Gatling Laser

Five times stronger than a Gatling Laser but must cool down to zero to be usable again after overheat. Hold down the fire button when overheated to force the built-in cooler to dissipate the heat as fast as possible. Range is 10km only and need a ship over 200.000t like Andromeda or Liners. Can mine asteroids also.
