Equipment Quirium Fuel Storage Unit

Scooped Quirium fuel storage container for 7ly of fuel. Unit expands to fill 1t of cargo space as fuel is added. Connection is one-way - fuel stored in these containers cannot be fed back into ship fuel reserves.
