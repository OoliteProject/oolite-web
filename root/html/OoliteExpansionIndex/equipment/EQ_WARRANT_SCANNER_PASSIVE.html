Equipment Warrant Scanner

Equipment that automatically scans a targeted ship for bounties outstanding in other systems and sectors. Must be with 15kms of target to use, and use will generally be viewed as a hostile act. Non-combatant targets will not be scanned automatically when your ship's weapons are turned off.
