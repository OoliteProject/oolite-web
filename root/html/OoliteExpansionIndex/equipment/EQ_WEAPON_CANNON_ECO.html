Equipment Eco Cannon

Energy saver successor of Lance & Ferman Military Laser Cannon with double damage, using engine exhaust gases for small energy usage and half range (5 km). Designed for ships with separated multiple laser mounts to avoid depleting all energy banks before overheat. Need a ship with at least 130t mass like Cobra Mark III.
