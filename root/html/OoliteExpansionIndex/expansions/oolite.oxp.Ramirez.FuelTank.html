Oolite OXP

Fuel Tank v2.2

Ramirez, 31 May 2008

This is a small equipment OXP that allows players to buy additional tanks of witchdrive fuel, using them to refill the ship's main tanks during flight.  Fuel transfer is achieved by selecting and activating a tank in the same way as for a mine.

Each tank contains 3.0 light years' worth of fuel, at a recommended price of 70cr including installation costs. 


Changes from v2.0: Altered AI scripting for compatibility with Oolite v1.70.

Changes from v1.0:  Altered role in shipdata to prevent NPCs from using the equipment and so giving the player free fuel.
