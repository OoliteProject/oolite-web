Description Makes standard missiles more useful by rebalancing missiles' relationship with ECM. Press 'i' for more information. - - - - - - - - - - The ECM is commonly equipped by non-player ships, often neutering the standard missile - one of the few weapons a new pilot can afford. By making ECM rarer, all missiles become more useful; in order to create a similar effect for the player, ECM is less commonly available and more fragile. Hardened missiles are made less available and more expensive but there are two new missile types: defence (cheap) and torpedo (powerful).
