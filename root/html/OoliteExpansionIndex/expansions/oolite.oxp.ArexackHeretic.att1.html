readme.txt

This expansion pack introduces new ships - the Gaundlet Armoured Transport Type 1 and the Gaundlet Armoured Escort Viper.

Convoys of well armed craft ferry gold and other valuables through the space lanes. Only a pirate with an Iron Ass would dream of attempting a robbery!

The OXP was written by Arexack Heretic.
