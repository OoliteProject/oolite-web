Overview
========

The Fer-de-Pai is an improved remake of the Ferdepai OXP (2006) which, due to a missing license, can not be modified. The ship is
using the model and modified textures from Deepspace and CaptSolo.
 
Credits
=======

johnsmith (original concept)
Deepspace (model and textures)
