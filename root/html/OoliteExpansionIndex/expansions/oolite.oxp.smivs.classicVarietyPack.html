Classic Variety Pack

This OXZ adds twenty more textures to The Classic Ships, to give a richer and more varied spread of designs. 
These have a similar style to the main OXZ and will complement both the Addition and Replace versions. Some share a colour-scheme with existing ships to give the impression of 'corporate livery' - a shipping line which uses a variety of ships but with a common appearance.
They share the same specification as the standard ships and should appear with a similar frequency, except one or two which will occur only very rarely - little surprises to look out for. 

Included are:-
1 x Adder
1 x Anaconda 
1 x Asp
2 x Boa
1 x Boa 2
1 X Cobra Mk I
1 x Cobra Mk III
1 x FerDeLance
1 x Gecko
1 x Krait
2 x Python
3 x Transporter
2 x Worm
