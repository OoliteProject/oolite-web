readme/readme.txt

        
Staer9 shipset


An oxp for Oolite that adds the ships by Staer9 to the no shadered Ooniverse
 
Overview:
Staer9's ships as companions to  Griff's ship models.

In this OXP, only the textures were modified so they render well on GPU's that do not support OpenGL shading language.
The clever procedure how to convert was developed by Captain SOLO.

Requirements:
Tested with Oolite 1.80. No dependencies. 

Installation:
After unzipping, move the folder, "Noshaders_Z_GrOovy_CobraMk3_Variety_Pack_v1.0.1.oxp" to the AddOns Folder of your Oolite installation. 

Credits or rather still "Standing on shoulders of giants":
* Staer9 for his really neat ship models for his shipsets.

* Keeper and now Spara keeping the shipset alive and update it for newer Oolite versions.

* Griff for his marvelous ship designs

* Captain Solo, who invented a clever way to convert them for computers with shaderless GPUs and helped me 
  extensively to explain how it was done for his noshader shipset (now default ships for 1.80)

Staer9's Shipset.oxp
----------------------------------------------------

An oxp tested and working in Oolite v1.75 and higher, it should work with some earlier versions, but isn't officially supported.

Installing and Uninstalling this oxp
------------------------------------
To install, simply copy the oxp folder into your oolite AddOns folder, next time you start the game the ship will be available.
to Uninstall, move or delete the oxp folder from your oolite AddOns folder, next time you start the game the ship will be gone.

OXP Requirements: none at all
