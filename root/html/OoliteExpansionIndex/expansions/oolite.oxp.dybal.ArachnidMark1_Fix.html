Readme.txt

This OXP fixes the use of CARGO_RANDOM in the cargo_type field of the ship's shipdata definition.

Such use made the Oolite populator consider the ship a cargo pod.

It's done through a shipdata-overrides since the original OXP license doesn't allow direct modification and update of the original OXP.
