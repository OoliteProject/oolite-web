The D.T.T Galaxy Liner



Basically a Boa with a little more speed, but a little less cargo capacity. JazHaz requested a ship that looked more like a passenger liner than a cargo hauler (http://aegidian.org/bb/viewtopic.php?f=2&t=15733&hilit=passenger#p210008), so this is my take on it. Though as far as I know, there is no way to give this "permanent" passenger berths... It will be up to the user to decide how it is to be used.

To install, just unzip and place the .oxp folder into your AddOns folder.
