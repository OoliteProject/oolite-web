Ship OXP: Copperhead Mk II

Overview:
SoloTek Industries has been granted receivership of all material assets once the property of Xeceesian Shipyards. The original Copperhead was a rediscovery from a century ago. The Copperhead Mk II is larger and faster than it's predecessor, and has twice it's cargo capacity. If well equipped, it can stand it's own in a fight. That is if her commander exercises good judgment. This ship is an excellent choice for the beginning trader or experienced bounty hunter. Sadly, a few unscrupulous sales persons did not check GalCop's legal records on every potential customer. 

The Copperhead Mk II can be found at tech level six systems and higher.
