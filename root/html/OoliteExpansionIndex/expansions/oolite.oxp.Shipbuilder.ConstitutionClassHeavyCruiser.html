Constitution Class Heavy Cruiser OXP by Shipbuilder.

A new ship from GalTech Industries. 

--------------------------------------------------------------
Ship:

A Constitution Class Heavy Cruiser (As per the Starship Enterprise in the Star Trek 2 and 3 motion pictures) - This OXP adds a Constitution Class Heavy Cruiser as a NPC ship. This class of starship are constructed by The GalTech Industries Corporation exclusively for Starfleet and are designed to combine both long range exploration with a substantial military capability. Due to the exclusivity deal in place with Starfleet and the huge production cost these vessels are not available as player ships.

Requirements -

This OXP requires the BigShips OXP produced by Thargoid and Eric Walch to also be installed (A copy is included in this OXP for convenience).
The OXP has been developed for Oolite V1.76 however it should also be upward compatible with future releases.
