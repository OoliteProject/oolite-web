Description Vital information packages for new pilots to help master Oolite. On the Expansions Manager, press \"i\" for the list. This pack does nothing after installed, so you can remove it and then also remove any unwanted packages. Installs: Ship's Library with ship's manual, Technical Reference Library (identifies many ships), XenonUI (identifies many key commands), GalCop Galactic Registry (guide to the galaxy), Galactic Almanac (guide to local system), Market Observer (vital market information), Hints (overhear useful tittle-tattle about combat, trade, etc. at the local bar) & Vimana-X HUD (flashes warnings for Incoming Missiles, High Temperature, Low Altitude, Energy & Shields). Optional: HUD selector. Warning: do not install this collection if you plan to read through the entire oxp/oxz list (1,100+!) and can spend enough time to build up your very unique Ooniverse.
