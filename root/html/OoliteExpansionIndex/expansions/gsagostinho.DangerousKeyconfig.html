Dangerous Keyconfig
===================

Version: 1.2
Required Oolite Version: 1.84
Author: Gilberto Agostinho (gsagostinho)
License: CC-BY-NC-SA 3.0

About
=====

Alternative keyboard bindings similar to what is used in Elite Dangerous. Use 'a' and 'd' to yaw, 'TAB' for fuel injector, 't' for ident target, 'u' for weapons online/offline, 'PgUp' and 'PdDn' for scanner zoom, 'r' for target missile, 'R' for untarget missile, 'g' for activating primable equipment, '_' for cargo rotation, 'End' for dumping cargo, 'G' for galatic hyperdrive, '!' for fast activattion of equipment A (defensive) and '@' for fast activattion of equipment B (offensive).
