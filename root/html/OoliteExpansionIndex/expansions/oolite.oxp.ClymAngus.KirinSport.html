An unofficial variant of the Kirin CV, the Sport is the smallest
and fastest - and cheapest - iteration of the Kirin class freighter line.
A redesign of the Kirin spaceframe, the Sport was destined as a team support vehicle and/or
emergency SAR craft for the racing circuits, a role in which it succedes beyond expectations.
With this success, more were manufactured and placed on the common market, in a freighter configuration.

The Sport suffers the same incompatibilities with the docking computer as it's heavier siblings,
so the captian is warned to keep his (or her) manual docking skills honed, although the more compact Sport
should be easier to dock.
